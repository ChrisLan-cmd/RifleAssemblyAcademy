SAR 21 at a Glance

The SAR 21 (“Singapore Assault Rifle – 21st Century”) is Singapore’s bullpup assault rifle chambered for 5.56×45 mm NATO. Developed from 1995–1999 by MINDEF, the Singapore Army, and Chartered Industries of Singapore (now ST Engineering Land Systems), it replaced the M16S1 as the SAF’s standard service weapon in 1999.

Key Features

  • Sealed Reliability: Gas system and polymer parts resist salt, sand, and humidity.
  • Bullpup Ergonomics: Compact overall length without sacrificing barrel velocity; ambidextrous controls.
  • Built-in Stability: Integrated bipod and folding foregrip for rapid, steady aiming.
  • Optics-Ready: MIL-STD-1913 rail for scopes, lights, and other accessories.

Beyond Singaporean service, the SAR 21 is exported globally via ST Kinetics and, in the U.S., through its subsidiary VT Systems.
